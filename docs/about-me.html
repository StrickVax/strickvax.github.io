    Hello! I'm StrickVax, a passionate Computer Science student majoring in Data Science at UC Riverside. My journey began at Moreno Valley College, where I not only developed my foundational skills in C++ but also served as a Student Senator for 3 years.

    In my role as Student Senator, I've volunteered at various events and worked tirelessly to make sure that student voices were heard in faculty meetings. This experience has taught me valuable leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills that I apply to my work in technology.

    Though I have yet to embark on a professional internship, I have committed myself to hands-on learning by creating several engaging projects. My portfolio includes a DHTML Editor, a MERN-based Price Tracker, and a Python-driven Math Puzzle Solver. These projects showcase my growing skills and ambition to craft solutions that make a difference.

    Outside of academia and coding, I enjoy playing Team Fortress 2 and always look for ways to learn and grow in my field. I'm excited about the opportunities and challenges ahead, and I invite you to explore my projects.
